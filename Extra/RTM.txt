Project: Gmail Application

Description: Gmail: Login

FR001 : Login
        The gmail login page opens with the url in the browser: www.gmail.com. The username, password textbox and sign in button appears
		
FR001-a : Login valid
        Enter valid username and password. Click on sign in button. The account opens with inbox
		
FR001-b : Login invalid
        Enter invalid username & password. Click on sign in button. The account does not open and error message is displayed

FR001-c : Login blank
        Username & password blank. Click on sign in button. The account does not open and error message is displayed

input fields: 
		Username: Alpha-numeric in lower case between 4 to 8 characters
		Password: Alphabet in lower case between 4 to 8 characters

Project: Verizon Wireless: Telecom project

Description: 

FR001 : Store locator
        The “Store locator” functionality appears on the top menu of home page of Verizon wireless.com
        Store locator locates the various Verizon wireless stores across US, based on the city/state chosen. Search is based on the two fields below:
        1. Enter zip-code: accepts 5 digit zip-code
        2. Choose a state: gives the list of states

		
FR001-a : zipecode invalid
        Enter invalid zip code, the store details are not displayed and the all the fields are reset
		
FR001-b : Zipecode valid or choose state
        Enter zipcode or select a state . Click on FIND STORES to display the results in below format:
        Address, Work Hours, Featured Services, workshop link


FR001-c : Zipecode & choose state blank
        the store details are not displayed and the all the fields are reset

input fields: 
		 1.Enter zip-code: accepts 5 digit zip-code
		 2.Choose a state: gives the list of states
